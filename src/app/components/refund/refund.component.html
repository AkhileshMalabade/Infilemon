Refund and Cancellation Policy

Introduction

At Smartup India Ventures ("Company," "we," "us," or "our"), we strive to provide excellent services and products to our customers. We understand that there may be circumstances necessitating refunds or cancellations. This policy explains the terms and conditions governing such requests.

Services Refund Policy

Refunds for services are generally not provided due to the nature of our business. However, refunds may be issued under the following circumstances:

  • Cancellation Before Project Start: If you cancel a service before work begins, you may be eligible for a partial or full refund, depending on preparatory work completed. Contact us promptly to discuss.
  • Incomplete Delivery: If we fail to complete a project due to unforeseen circumstances or cannot deliver the agreed scope, a partial refund may be provided based on work completed.
  • Mutual Agreement: Refunds may be issued through mutual agreement.

Refund requests must be submitted in writing within 7 days of the event that triggered the request. Email us at infilemon@gmail.com.

Product Refund Policy

Refunds for physical products are governed by the following:

  • Defective Products: Report any defects or damages within 7 days of receipt. A replacement or refund will be provided after verification.
  • Incorrect Orders: Notify us within 7 days of receiving an incorrect item for a replacement or refund.
  • Non-Refundable Items: Customized items (e.g., branded merchandise) are non-refundable unless a defect or error occurred on our part.

To initiate a return or refund, contact us at infilemon@gmail.com with order details.

Cancellations

  • Service Cancellations: Notify us as early as possible if you wish to cancel a service. Cancellations before work starts may be eligible for a refund per the "Services Refund Policy." Once work has begun, refunds may not be available depending on project progress.
  • Product Order Cancellations: Orders for physical products can be canceled before shipment. If already shipped, you may follow the product return process.

Refund Processing

Approved refunds will be processed within 14 business days via the original payment method. Processing times may vary depending on your bank or payment provider.
